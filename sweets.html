Sweets

Candy and Chocolate

LifeSavers Mints

When I say I love these mints--I mean it.
I eat about 10-15 a day and while I've tried different methods to
stop me from this practically-addiction, I just can't. I love the
taste of it and when they dissolve in my mouth, I feel a sense of
peace. I have the biggest pack at home and to go home to find my
mints staring back at me waiting for me to devour them, brings me joy.

Jolly Ranchers

My second favorite candy. Unlike the mints
though, I don't eat these as much as I'd like considering this is
actually candy but either way I love it. Mixed with all very
delicious flavors from strawberry and cherry to green apple and
blueberry; if I was allowed to- I would eat a bunch every day.

SNICKERS

My number one favorite chocolate. A chocolate
bar filled with caramel, nuts and nougats that feels like disney
land in your mouth. Unfortunately, I only eat this once in a while
but whenever I do, it's fantastic.

KitKat

When you hear that *crunch* sound, you know you've
entered heaven. I could sit and eat tons of kit kats for hours because
the two simple things in it--milk chocolate and light, crispy wafers
make it absolutely amazing. Kit kat is also the only chocolate my mom
likes so she buys it for me more often.

Candy and Chocolate

LifeSavers Mints

When I say I love these mints--I mean it. I eat about 10-15 a day and while I've tried different methods to stop me from this practically-addiction, I just can't. I love the taste of it and when they dissolve in my mouth, I feel a sense of peace. I have the biggest pack at home and to go home to find my mints staring back at me waiting for me to devour them, it brings me joy.
